About the Project

Chotobela is an online platform showcasing children’s clothing, toys, and lifestyle products. It is designed with a modern, user‑friendly interface to provide parents and families with a smooth shopping experience.

Features

  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
  • Product catalog with categories
  • Shopping cart and checkout flow
  • Clean navigation and modern UI
